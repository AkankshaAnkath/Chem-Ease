Clubs in Chemical Engineering Department

IICHE

Indian Institute Of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) is a confluence of streams of professionals from academia, research institute and industry. It provides them the appropriate forum for joint endeavors, hand-in-hand, to work for human being through application of chemical engineering and allied sciences.

INNOVATIVE DESIGN CLUB

The club provides a platform for young minds to experiment and innovate, and come up with something extra ordinary solutions for the present and futuristic challenging scientific and societal problems
